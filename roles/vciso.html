Key Process Areas

Seven AI-powered process areas that define your vCISO command center.

Security Posture Scoring

Continuous, real-time security posture assessment across every client environment. AI-driven scoring quantifies risk, benchmarks against industry standards, and tracks improvement over time.

Threat Intelligence Dashboard

Aggregated threat intelligence feeds correlated with your portfolio's attack surface. AI filters noise, prioritizes actionable threats, and maps indicators of compromise to your environments.

Compliance Gap Analysis

Automated mapping of your current controls against NIST, CIS, SOC 2, HIPAA, and other frameworks. Identify gaps instantly, get remediation recommendations, and track closure progress.

Incident Response Orchestration

AI-coordinated incident response workflows with automated runbooks, escalation paths, and real-time status tracking. Reduce mean time to containment and ensure consistent response quality.

Risk Register Management

Centralized risk register with AI-powered risk scoring, trend analysis, and mitigation tracking. Automatically surface emerging risks and correlate findings across your entire managed portfolio.

Vendor Security Assessment

Automated third-party risk assessments that pull live security data, evaluate vendor posture, and generate risk ratings. Continuous monitoring replaces point-in-time questionnaires.

Security Awareness Campaigns

AI-personalized security training with adaptive phishing simulations, behavior analytics, and engagement tracking. Measure effectiveness and continuously improve your human security layer.
