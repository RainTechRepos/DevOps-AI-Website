Key Process Areas

Seven AI-powered process areas that turn compliance chaos into continuous, automated audit readiness.

📋

Evidence Collection Engine

Automated, continuous evidence gathering across all systems. AI validates completeness, freshness, and relevance — evidence is always audit-ready.

🔄

Multi-Framework Mapping

CMMC, SOC 2, HIPAA, and custom framework controls mapped with overlap detection. Implement once, satisfy many — AI identifies shared controls.

📊

Compliance Dashboard

Real-time compliance posture across all frameworks and clients. AI scores maturity, tracks progress, and flags at-risk areas.

🔍

Control Monitoring

Continuous automated verification that controls are implemented, operating, and effective. AI detects drift and alerts on failures.

⚡

Remediation Planning

AI-generated remediation plans with prioritized actions, estimated effort, and dependency mapping. Fix the most impactful gaps first.
