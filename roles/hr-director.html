Before

Key Process Areas

Seven AI-powered process areas that turn complex people operations into streamlined, intelligent workforce management.

🚀

Automated Onboarding

End-to-end onboarding automation — account provisioning, equipment assignment, orientation scheduling, and welcome workflows. New hires are productive from day one.

📊

Workforce Analytics

Real-time retention trends, turnover prediction, engagement scoring, and diversity metrics. AI identifies risks and recommends interventions.

Automated access provisioning and deprovisioning synced with HR events. Join-move-leave workflows ensure compliance and security.
