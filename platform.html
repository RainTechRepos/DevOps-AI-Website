Intelligence

AI Orchestration Engine

Multi-model routing, chain-of-thought reasoning, and safety gates — ensuring every AI action is explainable, auditable, and approved.

Multi-Model Routing

Cost-effective routing via Azure OpenAI — the engine selects the optimal model for each task, balancing capability, cost, and latency.

Chain-of-Thought Reasoning

Every AI decision includes a transparent reasoning chain — visible to operators and archived for compliance auditing.

Safety Gates

Human-in-the-loop approval for all significant actions. Configurable escalation thresholds with RBAC-based authorization.
