The Platform

What is DevOps AI?

An enterprise-grade, AI-orchestrated MSP control plane that automates and augments the full operational lifecycle of a Managed Service Provider — transforming traditional delivery into an automation-first, People, Period. DevOps-as-a-Service model with human-in-the-loop safety at every critical decision.

AI Orchestration Layer

Multi-model routing with chain-of-thought reasoning. Every action is logged, explainable, and auditable — no black-box decisions.

Human-in-the-Loop Safety

Four-tier HITL gate system (L0-L3) on every process area. AI proposes, humans approve — from fully automated monitoring to human-only legal review.

Zero-Trust Architecture

All data stays in the client's Azure tenant. No traffic leaves the VNet. Public AI APIs are blocked at the firewall. Private endpoints everywhere.
