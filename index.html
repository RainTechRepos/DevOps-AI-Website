What is DevOps AI?

An enterprise-grade, AI-orchestrated MSP control plane that automates and augments the full operational lifecycle of a Managed Service Provider — transforming traditional delivery into an automation-first, people-first DevOps-as-a-Service model.

AI Orchestration Layer

Multi-model routing with chain-of-thought reasoning. Every action is logged, explainable, and auditable — no black-box decisions.

Unified Control Plane

Orchestrates tools your MSP already uses — NinjaRMM, ConnectWise, ServiceNow, Intune, Wazuh, and more — through a single AI layer.

Zero-Trust Architecture

All data stays in the client's Azure tenant. No traffic leaves the VNet. Public AI APIs are blocked at the firewall. Private endpoints everywhere.
