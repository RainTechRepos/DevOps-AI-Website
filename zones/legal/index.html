Legal

Legal Operations & Risk

⚖️ 7 Process Areas 📄 Contract Intelligence 🔍 Regulatory Automation

Legal is the legal operations and risk management hub of every MSP. DevOps AI transforms manual contract review and reactive compliance into an AI-powered legal operations platform — automating contract analysis, tracking regulatory changes, and managing legal holds with precision.

With 7 dedicated process areas, the Legal zone covers the complete legal operations spectrum: from AI-assisted contract review and regulatory filing through IP portfolio management, dispute tracking, and privacy impact assessments. Every legal event generates intelligence that reduces risk and improves compliance.

Cross-zone integration means your legal operations don't exist in isolation. Compliance requirements flow into GRC for audit readiness, contract terms inform Accounting billing processes, and organizational policies align with Legal governance standards — all automatically connected.

Scenarios

Real-World Use Cases

Scenario 1

When 50 vendor contracts need renewal review in a quarter...

Contract Review & Analysis automatically extracts key terms, flags unfavorable clauses, compares against benchmarks, and generates executive summaries — reducing review time from hours to minutes per contract.

Scenario 2

When a new data privacy regulation affects multiple clients...

Privacy Impact Assessments automatically identifies affected systems and data flows, Regulatory Filing Automation prepares compliance documentation, and Compliance Documentation generates audit-ready evidence packages.

Scenario 3

When a former employee disputes their termination...

Dispute Resolution Tracking creates a structured case file, Legal Hold Management preserves all relevant communications and documents, and the system tracks deadlines and generates status reports for legal counsel.
