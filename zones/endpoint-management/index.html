Endpoint Management

Unified Device Intelligence

💻 10 Process Areas 🔧 Unified Device Control 📊 Predictive Maintenance

Endpoint Management is the device intelligence backbone of every MSP. DevOps AI transforms scattered RMM tools and manual patching into a unified, AI-driven endpoint operations platform — automating deployments, predicting failures, and maintaining compliance across every managed device.

With 10 dedicated process areas, the Endpoint Management zone covers the full device lifecycle: from automated patching and software deployment through inventory reconciliation, configuration compliance, and mobile device management. Every endpoint generates telemetry that continuously improves operational intelligence.

Cross-zone integration means your endpoints don't operate in isolation. Security vulnerabilities flow seamlessly into Security Operations, network issues trigger Network Ops workflows, and device health feeds into Service Desk ticket intelligence — all without manual handoffs.

Scenarios

Real-World Use Cases

Scenario 1

When a critical zero-day patch drops at midnight...

AI risk-scores the vulnerability, identifies affected endpoints across all clients, stages patches with automatic rollback protection, and deploys during maintenance windows — all before technicians arrive.

Scenario 2

When 200 laptops need Windows migration...

OS Image Management builds tested golden images with driver injection, Software Deployment schedules rollouts by site with bandwidth throttling, and Device Lifecycle updates asset records automatically.

Scenario 3

When configuration drift threatens compliance...

Configuration Compliance detects the drift instantly, correlates it with recent changes, triggers automated remediation, and generates an audit trail — satisfying compliance requirements without manual intervention.
