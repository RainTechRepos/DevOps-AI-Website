Relationships

Client Success Automation

🤝 11 Process Areas 💡 Client Success Intelligence 📈 Revenue Growth Engine

Relationships is the client success engine of every MSP. DevOps AI transforms scattered CRM data and manual client management into a unified, AI-driven relationships platform — predicting churn before it happens, automating onboarding, and generating QBR-ready insights from operational data.

With 11 dedicated process areas, the Relationships zone covers the entire client lifecycle: from AI-powered lead scoring and onboarding through health monitoring, QBR generation, and contract renewal intelligence. Every client interaction generates insights that continuously improve retention and growth.

Cross-zone intelligence means your client relationships don't operate in a silo. Service desk satisfaction flows seamlessly into health scoring, financial data enriches renewal predictions, and analytics trends feed into QBR content — all without manual data gathering.

Scenarios

Real-World Use Cases

Scenario 1

When a key client's health score drops suddenly...

Client Health Scoring detects the decline from combined ticket volume, CSAT drops, and engagement reduction. The system alerts the account manager, generates a recovery playbook, and schedules a proactive check-in — before the client considers leaving.

Scenario 2

When QBR season arrives for 50 clients...

QBR Generation automatically compiles operational metrics, satisfaction trends, and strategic recommendations for each client. Account managers review and customize rather than building from scratch — reducing weeks of prep to hours.

Scenario 3

When a new client signs a managed services agreement...

Onboarding Orchestration creates a complete project plan, assigns tasks across teams, sets up monitoring and integrations, and tracks milestones — ensuring nothing falls through the cracks during the critical first 90 days.
